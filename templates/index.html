Why this exists

"Junior Developer — 0 to 2 years experience" that somehow requires 5 years of React, AWS, Docker, and Kubernetes. We've all seen it. JobLens was built to measure it — not just complain about it. Every job post gets parsed for experience requirements and skill demand. Every story submitted makes the picture clearer.

How it works

01

Scrape

Job listings are pulled from public sources and parsed for experience requirements, skills, and job type automatically.

02

Analyse

Experience inflation is detected, top skills are ranked, and patterns across remote vs on-site roles are surfaced.

03

Collect

Anyone can submit their own ATS rejection or hiring story. The data gets richer with every submission.
